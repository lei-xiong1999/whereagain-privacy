Common questions

Why does the app ask for photo access?
Where Again? builds memory rounds from your own photos. All analysis happens on your device — your photos never leave it.

Will it change my photos?
Only if you choose to. When you restore a location to a photo that's missing one, it's written to your library only after you explicitly confirm it.

How do I manage my subscription?
"Where Again? Plus" is billed through your Apple account. Manage or cancel it any time in Settings → your name → Subscriptions on your device.
